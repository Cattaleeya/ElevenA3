Responsibilities:

You will report to the product design lead and the role will require you to work autonomously and within a small team. The role will entail design testing, creation, and manufacturing solutions to our hardware component side of our product. This role will provide the successful candidate with opportunities to develop true commercial mechanical engineering experience and showcase deep understanding of most aspects of the field. You will be exposed to all kinds of mechanical, electrical, and even pneumatic applications upon creating devices to be used within a residential environment. The successful applicant will be able to coordinate and deliver partial and complete engineering projects from beginning and until end. This will include concept planning and drawings, estimate costings and calculations, Australian standards certification, fabrication of hardware and testing of final product including creating a testing program.

  • Able to create and provide reports on product design showing calculations ensuring the design complies with all Australian standards
  • Able to showcase and confirm all design drawings
  • Able to develop the hardware with quality materials through design standards
  • Able to design a quality testing program and facilitate testing
  • Consult and present findings, reports and demonstrations of hardware developed including completed and incremental stages.
  • Adjust design accordingly with product development team
  • Create reports based on investigation into design issues and provide advice on remedial options
  • Work in tandem with the product development team and the project manager to evaluate the scope of work needed, quality requirements, Australian standards before moving to manufacturing phase.
  • You will be required to travel either domestic or international from time to time to meet with prospective stakeholders including manufacturing partners and material suppliers.

Key Competencies:

  • Degree in Mechanical Engineering or equivalent qualification
  • A high level of competency in 3D modelling and drawing standards. Proven works highly desirable
  • Key industry knowledge of all regulatory requirements for industry development
  • Strong communication skills
  • Strong ethical practice in both design and work
  • Drive to take pride in your work. The Eleven only want to create the best quality product for their market.
