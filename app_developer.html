App Developer iOS and Android Platforms

You will receive:

  • A higher end laptop with high processing capabilities for you to complete tasks
  • Company iPhone
  • Generous travel arrangements, accommodation and meals allowances when travelling for work
  • Work from home unless required to travel upon occasion
  • 12-week contract, $800 per day + Superannuation P.A
  • Flexible working environment
  • Birthday Leave
  • Employee Assistance Program
  • Free parking
  • Access to Gym discount membership

Responsibilities:

You will report to the product design lead and the role will require you to work autonomously and within a small team. The role will entail creation of our company’s software for iOS and Android platforms along with the specific design functionality outlined in the project. Responsibilities

  • Work alongside the design and engineering team developing our iOS and Android application to integrate with our hardware component of our product
  • Create reports based on outcomes achieved and outline progress reports to the project manager
  • Collaborate with the product design team to determine functionality outcomes for the app
  • Work with the testers to determine possible faults and errors and create solutions
  • Determine the scope of the work involved including cost estimates and consult with project manager about timeframe

Key Competencies:

  • Demonstrated experience in Kotlin/Java
  • Demonstrated experience in Swift
  • Extensive experience developing native iOS and Android applications
  • Experience in Android and iOS frameworks and the quality standards that apply to the development
  • Experience publishing apps to the Google play and App store
  • Demonstrated Strong communication skills
Desirable Skills:
  • Experience working in collaborative environments
  • Have creative methodologies and is committed to creating simple, sophisticated product quality
  • HTML5 and JavaScript highly desired
