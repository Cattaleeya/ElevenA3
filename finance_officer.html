You will receive:

  • Competitive salary $85,000 + Super P.A
  • Access to Gym discount membership
  • Birthday Leave

  • Employee Assistance Program
  • Company Laptop & iPhone

Responsibilities:

As a financial officer at the eleven, you will be reporting to the Project Manager and provide financial advice to identify opportunities which will include further development of financial reporting, budget, risk assessment and analysis.

  • Assist with the completion of the month-end cycle within clearly defined timelines, including accounting for key areas such as revenue, remuneration expenses, operating expenses, accruals, debtors, cash & fixed assets
  • Ensure allocated balance sheet reconciliations are completed timely with appropriate resolution of all reconciling items
  • Establish and maintain audit trails for allocated revenue/expense streams and balance sheet accounts
  • Provide support for the half-year, interim & year-end audit processes
  • Provide advice to budget
  • Ensure payroll function is well managed and operated properly
  • Ensure accounts receivable are collected on a timely basis
  • Maintain finance system and chart of accounts
  • Maintain relationship with bank

Key Competencies:

  • CA or CPA qualified with 2/3 years of relevant experience
  • Strong technical skills and experience in Finance and Accounting
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment
  • Demonstrated ability to communicate effectively at all levels both verbal and written
  • SAP skills preferable
