Team Profile

Catt She/Her

I am 43 years old from Sydney, NSW. I like to play jigsaw puzzles, listening to music, and learning to play guitar online.

I have been interested in IT since I moved to work in Australia. During this time, I had to work closely with the Engineers Teams on the construction site in Karratha, Western Australia and two of my engineer colleagues showed me how to solve the IT problems/complication and showed me how to map the network drive. Besides, my former boss always upgraded his iPhone, iPad; however, he didn’t know how to connect to the company server so I would help him with that. And because of these scenarios my interest in the IT field was generated. Besides, I have always been curious about it on how it works, how they build the applications; and how coding is involved, etc. In addition, before I knew what I really want to do in the future, I also completed a bachelor’s degree of Arts, Advanced Diploma in Computing, Cer IV in Business Administration, Cer IV in Work Health and Safety and Cer II in Document Controller and Lead Auditor Certification for ISO 9001, ISO 14000 and OHSAS 18001. Therefore, do I have experiences in IT?, the answer is No for me and this is my first time to try the IT course and follow my dream.

Liz She/Her

I am a 36yo single parent living in regional Victoria, AU. I left high school early to study Clothing Production and Manufacturing, while also completing retail certificate working at an auto parts store. After a few years I transferred my role to another auto store in the Northern Territory, and additionally started working at a retail superstore in the computers department. The computers department had their own repair centre, and I found the work they did there interesting. I often asked questions about work they were doing and learned a few things which made me want to use my computer for more than just email. I enjoyed working there and had planned to return after parental leave, but I ended up moving back to Victoria once my first child was born.

I am a Skate Victoria accredited roller derby coach and skater for my local roller derby club. I enjoy reading when I have the time, I like playing Roblox and Minecraft with my kids, but am also a fan of the Final Fantasy series and used to play a large amount of League of Legends (though very badly). I find gardening enjoyable and grow a variety of flowers and foods, though my plants do not always survive, and whoever said you cannot kill a succulent was wrong.

When I became a ‘stay at home mum’ I started teaching myself HTML, CSS, and PHP as a hobby so I could customise blogging platforms such as Blogspot and WordPress. That lead me to becoming interested in web design and online businesses. I worked as a freelancer and contractor setting up WordPress websites, content writing and managing online communities (before Facebook). Wanting to gain formal qualifications I began studying and completed a Cert 3 in E-business, partially completed a Bachelor of Arts in Internet Communications, and partially completed a Diploma in Website Development while looking after my 4 children at home. Recently I completed a Cert IV in Business and then decided it was time to come back to IT.

Max He/Him

I am 18 years old from Sydney, NSW. I thoroughly enjoy gaming and streaming, along with playing rugby and going to the gym.

My interest in IT comes from attending a UTS work experience course, within this course I was shown how critical IT security is, One of the teachers at UST showed us how he helps implement systems and firewalls to prevent hackers, along with increasing the company’s security he has gotten paid by major companies such as apple to try and hack through there security firewalls as steal critical information, he then goes and shows them how he did it so they can improve their security. Thus, by doing this course, I hope it will help me find a career in cyber security, I believe doing this course is a great way to get myself familiar with the necessary requirement that could be needed within the industry, I don’t have any professional experience, but I pick things up quickly.

Nat She/Her

40+, 2 x girls (22,16), I am Chilean, I live in Pakenham, Victoria. Play piano, in my spare time, I am doing my uni course ( either homework or study ) I am also studying to gain my CCNA certification

I love IT as I like to know how things work, and I love the challenge that computers provide, you can have the such a variation of uses for them, and the specifications which is awesome. I am a movie buff so watching our the technology and movies progressed since the early 90’s has really attracted me to computers. Being the person “in the chair” or the technician you call for help. I achieved my Cert 4 in IT networking in 2010 and my Advanced Diploma in Computer Systems & Engineering and Security (Cyber/Networking) in 2015, I have been working at Officeworks for almost 10 year, I also teach basic/mid-level networking at Chisholm TAFE since 2015 (sessional), I also work at Geeks2U as an on-site / remote IT technician. Each of these jobs gives me separate skill sets, eg directly from the customers / business at Officeworks and Geeks2U purchasing the IT equipment / toys and needing to get them to work together, and new equipment coming out and then playing / using them to then having the students at TAFE and building on their gaps in knowledge. These jobs also give me the patience and user interaction to be able to help the customers / clients / student’s needs which are all different too.

Sam He/Him

I am 30 years old from Melbourne, Vic. In my spare time, I like to spend time with my partner Chloe and my two Dachshunds Maple and Hue. Aside from this, I spend a lot of my free time learning about different things on the internet and playing video games.

I currently work for a chemical company as a field technician. I work with others in the company to develop methods of use for an array of dangerous chemicals with cyanide bases. This work would see me traveling both nationally and internationally conducting field studies to be used for chemical registration world wide. A milestone achievement for me is that I hold the record for the largest fumigation with Stabilized Hydrogen Cyanide (HCN) within Australia to date – 38,600 Cubic metres.

IT completely fascinates me. I love how computers work, the potential applications of computers, and how they can change people’s lives. I first became interested in computers when I was young playing a Super Nintendo. From there my school had some access to early computers and I grew up with the technology evolving around me – going from a 5 ¼ Inch floppy disc Macintosh, iMacs, Windows XP, now windows 10 and seeing DVDs be invented and then phased out. I have no IT experience, but I am keen to learn.
